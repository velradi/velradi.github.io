The Verdius serves as a repository of information for the Kingdom, all documental records pertaining to the key information about the universe and beyond. While the Verdius is considered a trusted source, the bias of the publishers of works within this repository should always be taken into account.

The reason for a new branch of the Verdius is due to the destruction of old records, while the old Verdius was recovered and pieced togethered from fragmented data, it was then decided that it would exist as a historical record of the universe before the sundering, hence the new Verdius repository.
