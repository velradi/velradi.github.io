A General Overview

The Planet of Verdial was once a tropical world but with the sundering it became a continental world, with each continient posessing its own unique climate and characteristics, the planet itself is located within the Infinitum sector, this planet is the seat of power for the rulers of the Kingdom, the Eradir family.

The dominant, but not sole, race on the planet is the Verdians, having by far the highest population and land ownership over any of the other races, but they are not the only race, elves, dwarves, gnomes, dragons and many others exist on this planet
