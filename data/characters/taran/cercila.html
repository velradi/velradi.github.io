  • True Description
  • Cercila Eradir, once Princess of the Kingdom of Verdial and Wrath of the Pantheon of Gods, being the last valid surviving member of the royal family, she was supposed to become Queen, but surrendered her role to Sara Taran, who became Queen of the Verdian Kingdom. Cercila came to the planet of Azeroth approximately 14,835 years ago, during the days of the Kaldorei empire, under the guise of a Kaldorei known as Asirion. From there she went on to found the region known Shalazir, now known as the Kaldorei Ariean, located within the southern part of Kalimdor, however this ended in ruin when a civil war broke out among the people, instead of slaughter those he once considered allies he instead lead his people across the sea, creating a new land known as Etchfield, a land meant to embody the perfection Verdial once did. She recreated their language, their culture and even bestowed Veridian Magic onto those who would assist in the defense of their lands.

    But Cercila had a wanderers heart, and because of this she appointed others to rule Etchfield while she journeyed the lands of Azeroth, exploring and fighting for the future of the world she now calls home, or at least the world she calls home temporarily.

    While no longer possessing the full command of the divine powers she once did as the Wrath, she still benefits from the blessings she recieved upon becoming the wrath, including the superhuman strength, speed and stamina, and also her ability to bestow lesser divine magic on others.


  • Powers and Abilities


  • Enhanced Strength

  • Cercila has strength that rivals that of the gods, while her raw strength is not as enhanced as her speed, it is still great enough for her to punch through solid Veridian Steel and also be capable of lifting objects that an ordinary Verdian would have great difficulty doing so, able to hurl objects far larger than her at great distances and also rend the earth with stomps. There are plenty of equals to her in terms of raw strength but that wouldn't be her most powerful asset.


  • Titanic Dexterity


  • While strength is not her defining characteristic speed definitely would be. She would posess great speed, capable of moving at breakneck speeds, even while not in the air she is able to instantly move across vast distances, teleporting and moving at speeds which ordinary vision would have trouble keeping up with.


  • Invulnerability


  • Cercila has an invulnerability to all physical threats, the only exception is from enchanted weaponry, and magic in general, however she still possesses a greater resistance to magic than the average individual, weaker spells and enchantments will still have a diminished effect on her, despite being more effective than unenchanted weaponry. This invulnerability is carried over to almost everything, being unable to be hurt by anything that doesn't contain a trace of magic, be that the ground beneath her or weaponry such as missiles and explosives, magic within weaponry such as those mentioned used to be more common before the sundering, back in the Kingdom times, but with the diminishment of magic overall and the loss of the Kingdom and it's technology, it's significantly more rare.


  • Endless Stamina


  • While not infinite in the truest sense of the word, Cercila posesses a seemingly endless reserve of stamina, with it taking truly unique and powerful enemies to tire her, but on average most opponents she comes up against would not even come close to doing so. She is also more than capable of taking massive hits and still pushing through, with even the magical attacks of gods rarely enough to stop her or bring her down.


  • Veridian Sensitive


  • Owing to her unique heritage and position as a once servant of the gods, Cercila would possess the ability to call upon the gods divine magic, though at a drastically reduced rate from her earlier days as the Wrath, still capable of minor acts with the magic, as well as being able to bestow this magic upon others, albeit at an even further reduced effect.


  • Warp Magic


  • While her connection to this magic was temporarily severed after the sundering due to the loss of the Warp Crystal, her connection was later restored and made innate via the Riftblade. Warp magic allows Cercila to bend space and instantaneously teleport across short distances, and long distances with greater effort, while much of the advanced warp magic techniques were lost, leading to very few other powers able to actually be harnessed by Cercila, at least for now.
