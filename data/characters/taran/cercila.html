{p:c}{col:d3d9d9}Hair that never stays a single shade for long and observant verdant irises, it is clear that even physically, her appearance evokes a sense of mystery. Cercila stands at the modest height of 5'3, a height not often seen in her fellow human kin. Her face would be one of untouched perfection, not a single blemish or alteration anywhere upon her features. Her ears are pointed, knife-like as is to be expected, her ears adorned by a matching pair of diamond earrings, located upon the lobe of her ears. Like the rest of her appearance her hair is pristine, nary a hair out of place, it trailing to reach just past the middle of her back.

Cercila appears to be a refined individual, one born into a life of splendour and absent of burden, however should she flex, one might realise how physically capable she is, despite seemingly any visible reason to bother with doing so, she retains a strict training regieme, as shown by her slim but toned figure.{/p}
