  • About
  • Pet Name: Lokir
  • A tanned thalassian male that stands at just over six feet tall. Dishevelled, dark brown hair rests atop his head, flowing down to reach just past his shoulders, despite this rugged appearance there would be nothing to indicate the hair would be in a poor state.

    His features are both striking and handsome, not often does a smile grace his features, his visage more often than not being a subdued one, betraying little in the way of emotions. The only thing that would tarnish his visage would be a scar that runs over his right eye, though given the eye is still functional, it is clear that it did not make contact with the eye itself.

    He stands at just above six feet tall, his frame a muscular one, but even so it still yields to the characteristic litheness found on many an elf, making his frame a slim, but muscular blend. His body is equally blemished, similar to his face, with various scars, of varying severity, present upon his form, though these are not often visible.


  • True Description
  • Cercila Taran, shadow self to Asirion, otherwise known as Velradi.

    He took this form when the original Aurethan fell in battle at the attack on Quel'thalas by the scourge, he has since used this form to keep an eye on Quel'thalas and it's interests, given their close proximity to Etchfield, with most of his time having been spent in the city.
