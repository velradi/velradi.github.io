  • About
  • Hair akin to that of a raven's feathers in colouration and a blindfold which obscures any distinguishing features of his irises, the man looks similar how he wishes to be portrayed, as a stalwart and controlled individual. Ariean stands at the towering height of 7'3 ft, a height that while impressive among many races of Azeroth, would not be that remarkable among his kin. His body is that of a large and domineering frame, muscular and athletic, the form of a disciplined individual who has clearly taken the time to make it so. His face and body stand at stark contrast with one another, his face, unblemished and flawless would be contrasted by his body, on which could be found many a scar to blemish it's pristine condition.

    Ariean appears to be a calm and collected presence, no matter the situation, be it within social situations or within battle, Ariean would maintain an impassive demenour, this affecting not just negative but also positive emotions, rarely would a smile grace this man's features. His ears and eyebrows are shorter than that of his kin in length, though it would not be readily apparent as to why.

    Ariean maintains an impassive expression at all times, no matter if he is idle, or occupied, while he is more than capable of handling himself within social situations, and isn't one to shy away from them, his impassive demenour can come across as cold, or uncaring.

    His words are rarely an improvement, often times being blunt and sometimes even completely unaware of the affects his words could have upon others, though given his attitude, conveying himself as rude is unlikely to be something he does intentionally.


  • Pet Name: Lokir
  • True Description
  • Ariean Eradir, shadow self to Asirion, otherwise known as Velradi.

    He took this form when he first came to Azeroth, undertaking it so he might interact with the dominant species at the time, the Kaldorei Empire, he has gone on to found Shalazir and Etchfield under the guise of Ariean Eradir, however most recently has lost control of this vessel due to needing to leave it in Etchfield in order to prevent corruption from spreading.
